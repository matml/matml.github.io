About the Conference

The UKIEG Annual Conference & AGM 2025 explores "The Impact of Net Zero on Health and the Environment". Join scholars, industry experts, and policymakers to discuss Indoor Environmental Quality (IEQ), sustainable design, advanced monitoring, and their connections with health outcomes. Abstract submissions close on 14 April 2025, with notifications by the end of April.
